About

Lettuce in the House is a health-focused food business dedicated to serving fresh, delicious meals centered around lettuce. Our specialty lies in crafting vibrant dishes that combine crisp, farm-fresh lettuce with a flavorful variety of fruits, lean meats, and wholesome ingredients. Whether you're pursuing a balanced diet, a fitness journey, or simply craving a refreshing and nutritious meal, Lettuce in the House offers a menu full of taste, color, and wellness. We believe that eating healthy doesn’t mean sacrificing flavor—and every bowl proves it.
